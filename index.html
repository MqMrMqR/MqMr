IPSW Installation Guide

Windows MacOS

MacOS Catalina 10.15 and Newer

  1. Select your device and download the IPSW file from the link above.
  2. Connect your device to your Mac.
  3. Open Finder and click on your device in the Finder Sidebar.
  4. While holding the "Option" key, click the "Update" button.
  5. Browse to where you downloaded the IPSW earlier and select it.
  6. Wait for Finder to update your device.

Older Versions

  1. Select your device and download the IPSW file from the link above.
  2. Connect your device to your Mac.
  3. Click on your device in iTunes.
  4. While holding the "Option" key, click the "Update" button.
  5. Browse to where you downloaded the IPSW earlier and select it.
  6. Wait for iTunes to update your device.
